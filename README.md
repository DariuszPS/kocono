# 🎨 Kocono

**The first tool to preserve your Kobo Colour highlights when exporting to Apple Notes.**

<br>

## ✨ What is Kocono?

Kocono lets you export highlights from your **Kobo Clara Colour** or **Kobo Libra Colour** e-reader to Apple Notes **while keeping all your colors intact**.

No more losing your carefully color-coded system. No subscriptions. No data uploads. Everything runs in your browser.

<br>

## 🎯 How it works

1. **Connect your Kobo** to your Mac via USB
2. **Upload KoboReader.sqlite** from the `.kobo` folder
3. **Download the AppleScript** file
4. **Run it** in Script Editor
5. **Done!** Your highlights are now in Apple Notes with colors preserved

<br>

## 🟡🟣🔵🟢 Color System

Your Kobo Colour highlights use 4 colors. Kocono preserves them all:

- 🟡 **Yellow** → Knowledge & Facts
- 🟣 **Purple** → Personal Reflections  
- 🔵 **Blue** → Action Items
- 🟢 **Green** → Questions & Ideas

*(You can use your own system - these are just examples)*

## 🆚 Why Kocono?

**Readwise** ($5.99-9.99/mo): Imports Kobo highlights BUT drops all colors ❌  
**Kobo Note Up** (free): Preserves colors BUT no Apple Notes export ❌  
**Kocono** (free): Kobo Colour import + color preservation + Apple Notes = ✅

<br>

## 🙋 FAQ

**Where is KoboReader.sqlite?**  
Connect your Kobo via USB → Open Finder → Your Kobo device → `.kobo` folder (press Cmd+Shift+. to show hidden files)

**Is my data safe?**  
Yes! Everything happens in your browser. Nothing is uploaded to any server.

**Can I customize the colors?**  
Currently the mapping is fixed, but custom categories are coming in v2.0.

**Does this work on Windows?**  
Not yet - AppleScript only works on macOS. Windows support planned for future versions.
